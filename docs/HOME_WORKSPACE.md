# Home Workspace v0.1

## Purpose

The Home Workspace helps the operator know exactly what to do next.

## MVP Cards

1. Ava Morning Brief
2. Today's Top Plays
3. Business Pulse
4. Attention Center

## Success Criteria

Within 30 seconds, the operator knows:

- What changed
- What matters
- What to do first

## Card 1: Ava Morning Brief

Displays:

- Greeting
- Summary of what changed
- Top opportunity
- Biggest risk
- Recommended focus

## Card 2: Today's Top Plays

Displays 3–5 high-value actions.

Each play includes:

- Title
- Reason
- Business outcome
- Suggested action

## Card 3: Business Pulse

Displays current business health:

- Business
- Relationships
- Pipeline
- Marketing
- Transactions

## Card 4: Attention Center

Displays only items that require attention:

- Opportunity
- Action Required
- Risk
- Celebration

## Design Rule

No charts in v0.1 unless they directly help the operator decide what to do next.
